FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Short answers for dealers checking whether the app fits their workflow.

Is this a used car dealer inventory app?

Yes. It is built for car dealers who need to track vehicles, photos, VIN, purchase price, asking price, status, expenses, and profit.

Can it replace my dealership spreadsheet?

Yes. The app keeps inventory, expenses, clients, sales, reports, and team access in one mobile workflow instead of separate spreadsheets and notes.

Does it work on iPad?

Yes. The App Store listing says the app is designed for iPad and supports iPhone and iPad.

Can I invite my team?

Yes. Team access lets owners invite employees and control what financial information they can see.

Can I track profit per vehicle?

Yes. You can connect purchase price, expenses, sale price, deposits, receivables, debts, and reports to understand real vehicle profit.

Can I track parts too?

Yes. The app includes parts inventory, batch costs, stock levels, and part sales.

Can I export reports?

Yes. You can generate professional PDF reports for sales, expenses, and profit.

Is it free to download?

Yes. Car Dealer Tracker is free to download on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
